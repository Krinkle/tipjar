Make sure the Tipjar extension button isn't hidden in Chrome's menu! If you don't see it next to the address bar, open Chrome's window, right-click the Tipjar icon, and select 'Show in toolbar'.

How Tipjar works

When the site you have open accepts direct contributions, you'll see a green money icon in your browser's toolbar. All you have to do is click it, and you'll be sent to the appropriate page. You can see it in action on corbin.io.

Tipjar currently supports three different donation methods. If the site has a meta tag that links to a donation page, Tipjar will always use that first. If that isn't available, Tipjar will check its internal list of sites that support donations for a match.

Finally, if direct contributions are unavailable, Tipjar wil check if the site supports Scroll. Scroll is a subscription service that removes advertisements from supported websites and gives them a cut of your subscription cost instead â€” similar to how YouTube Premium works.
